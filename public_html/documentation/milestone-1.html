PWP Milestone 1 Project


Purpose:

The purpose of the Price Tag Website is to be an information page on the features, benefits, and pricing for the DataTags Price Tag Software.

Audience:

My ideal target audience are Appliance and Electronics stores in need of business support software that helps them focus on the part of their business that makes them money.

Goal:

The goal of the website is to find and advertise the software to business owners who looking for a price tag program and to convert the interest into sales.

Persona

Name:

William Porter

Picture:

Age:

50

Job:

Appliance and Electronics Retail Store Owner

Technology:

Samsung smart phone with an Android OS on it. Various Window Operating Systems from OS 7 to 10. Computers have been upgraded or added as needed. Potentially Using Chrome, Firefox, Edge depending on what's loaded on which machine they are on.

Behaviors and Attitudes:

He is dedicated and focused on the day to day work of selling. There are many moving parts to make it all function properly and it is a constant tug of war on his attentions. He relies on multiple different support services and staff to keep the business profitable. Sometimes he is having to follow up and inspect the work that's been done to make sure it is adequately done. Because of this he prefers simple processes to complex ones.

Frustrations:

He has several support programs, but they require separate upkeep processes to maintain the product data, inventory, and pricing. It's hard to get help because the processes are complicated and involved. It makes employee retention important and advancement more difficult.

User Story:

As a small business owner, I wear many different hats. I need my day-to-day processes to be uncomplicated and easy to manage.
