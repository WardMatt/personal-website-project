PWP Milestone 1 Project


Purpose:

The purpose of the Price Tag Website is to be an information page on the features, benefits, and pricing for the DataTags Price Tag Software.

Audience:

My ideal target audience are Appliance and Electronics stores in need of business support software that helps them focus on the part of their business that makes them money.

Goal:

The goal of the website is to find and advertise the software to business owners who looking for a price tag program and to convert the interest into sales.
